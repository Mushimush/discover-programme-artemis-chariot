How to use

  • Find IP: Open Arduino Serial Monitor, reset ESP32-CAM, look for IP address
  • Connect: Enter http://[IP]/stream and click Connect
  • Select Folder: Choose where to save images (creates subfolders per class)
  • Capture: Press S or click button - saves immediately to folder
  • New Class: Change the class name, keep capturing
  • Goal: 100-200 images per object, varied angles/lighting
